Summer Salad with Fresh Berries and Chicken

This salad can use any fresh berries, protien, cheese, and dressing you prefer. I opted for fresh strawberries, blueberries, goat cheese, sunflower seeds, romain lettuce, oven roasted chicken breast, and basil honey emulsion to bring it all together

Ingredient List

  • Strawberries-- cleaned and cut into small wedges
  • Blueberries-- cleaned and kept whole
  • Sunflower seeds-- lightly roasted in the oven with a small amount of olive oil, salt, and pepepr
  • Goat cheese-- broken into crumbles
  • Romain lettuce-- washed, cut into small bite size pieces
  • Chicken breast-- fat trimmed off, seasoned with salt, pepper, and a pinch of cayenne. Roasted at 350F until internal temperature is at least 165F
  • Basil Honey Emulsion

Basil Honey Emulsion

  1. 1oz Fresh basil-- blanched in salted water, moisture squeezed out, and fully cooled
  2. 2 sprigs of parsley
  3. 1 sprig of rosemary
  4. 1/4C honey
  5. 1/4C Champagne vinegar
  6. 1/2C canola oil
  • add everything bbut oil to blender, blend until fully smooth.
  • Slowly add oil while blender is running to emulsify the mixture
  • Season to taste with salt, pepper, and sugar if necessary.
