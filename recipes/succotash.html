Succotash

Ingredients

  • Fresh Lima beans-- 2C
  • Fresh corn kernels-- 2C
  • Red Bell Pepper-- 1, diced small
  • Green Bell Pepper-- 1, diced small
  • Yellow Onion-- 1 medium size, finely diced
  • Garlic-- 2 cloves, minced
  • Olive Oil-- 2TBSP
  • Salt and Pepper-- To taste
  • Parsley (optional)-- For garnish

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heart in a large skillet
  2. Add onion and garlic; saute until fragrant and onion is translucent
  3. Add bell peppers and cook until soft
  4. Stir in lima beans and corn; cook until hot, around 5-10 minutes
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and garnish with parsley
