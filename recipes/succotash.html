Succotash

Ingredients

  • Fresh Lima beans-- 2C
  • Fresh corn kernels-- 2C
  • Red Bell Pepper-- 1, diced small
  • Green Bell Pepper-- 1, diced small
  • Yellow Onion-- 1 medium size, finely diced
  • Garlic-- 2 cloves, minced
  • Olive Oil-- 2TBSP
  • Salt and Pepper-- To taste
  • Parsley (optional)-- For garnish
